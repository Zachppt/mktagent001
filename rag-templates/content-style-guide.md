## Core Writing Principles

1. **Lead with the answer** — never bury the point. State the conclusion, then explain it.
2. **Specific beats general** — "3,200 active nodes across 47 countries" beats "a global network"
3. **One idea per paragraph** — if a paragraph covers two things, split it
4. **Active voice** — "the network processes X" not "X is processed by the network"
5. **Cut the last paragraph** — most blog posts end one paragraph too late

## Blog Post Structure by Type

### GUIDANCE (How-to, Tutorial)
```
H1: How to [DO THE THING] with [YOUR_PROJECT]
[Intro: State what the reader will be able to do after reading — 2 sentences]
[Prerequisites: What they need before starting]
H2: Step 1 — [Action]
[Content]
H2: Step 2 — [Action]
[Content]
H2: Common Issues
[2-3 FAQs]
[Closing: What they've accomplished + next step]
```

### PEAK_INSIGHT (Thought leadership)
```
H1: [Bold claim or unexpected observation]
[Intro: The setup — what's happening in the market/tech that makes this important]
H2: [First major point]
H2: [Second major point]  
H2: [Third major point or counterargument addressed]
H2: What This Means for [YOUR_VERTICAL]
[Closing: Your take — direct, not hedged]
```

### UPDATE (Product / milestone announcement)
```
H1: [Feature Name] is Live — [What It Does in 5 Words]
[Intro: One paragraph — what launched, why it matters]
H2: What's New
[Bullet list of specific changes/features]
H2: How to [Use It / Access It]
[2-3 step instructions or link to docs]
H2: What's Next
[One sentence tease of what follows]
```

### SOCIAL_TREND (Reacting to a trend)
```
H1: What [TREND] Means for [YOUR_VERTICAL / PROJECT]
[Intro: Acknowledge the trend briefly, then pivot to your angle]
H2: Why This Matters
H2: [YOUR_PROJECT]'s Position / Take
H2: What You Can Do [or: What We're Building]
[CTA: Follow along / join / read more]
```

### ANNOUNCEMENT (Partnership, listing, launch)
```
H1: [YOUR_PROJECT] x [PARTNER] — [What It Means in 5 Words]
[Intro: The what and the why — 2 paragraphs]
H2: What This Means for [COMMUNITY / USERS]
[Specific benefits, features, or access changes]
H2: How It Works
[Technical or process detail if relevant]
[Quote from partner if available]
[CTA: Try it / learn more / join]
```

## Video Script Structure

```
[0:00–0:05] HOOK — One punchy statement or question that makes them stop scrolling
[0:05–0:30] PROBLEM/CONTEXT — What's broken or what's happening in the space
[0:30–END] CORE — Main content (demo, explanation, story)
[LAST 10s] CTA — One action: follow, visit link, try it, join community
```

**Scene note format**:
```
[SCENE 1: 0:00–0:05]
Visual: [describe what's on screen — be specific enough for a designer]
VO/Script: "[Exact spoken words]"
```

## Approved Writing Patterns

<!-- Things that work well — pulled from past top-performing content -->
- Opening with a counterintuitive statistic: "[X]% of [industry] projects fail at [specific point]. Here's why."
- Concrete comparisons: "The difference between X and Y is like [analogy]."
- Short declarative statements between longer paragraphs to create rhythm.

## Patterns to Avoid

- Starting a post with "In today's world..." or "In the rapidly evolving landscape..."
- Ending with "exciting times ahead" or "stay tuned for more updates"
- Passive voice: "it was decided that" / "users are able to"
- [Any specific phrases your team considers off-brand]

## SEO Metadata Standards

Every blog post must include:
- **Meta title**: Primary keyword in first 60 characters
- **Meta description**: 150–160 characters, includes primary keyword, ends with an action
- **Slug**: lowercase-hyphenated, max 5 words, includes primary keyword
- **Alt text**: every image needs descriptive alt text (not "image1.png")
