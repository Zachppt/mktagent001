## What Makes an Ideal Ecosystem Partner

### Must Have
- [ ] Operates in the same chain ecosystem: [e.g. Arbitrum, Ethereum mainnet, Solana]
- [ ] Complementary product — not competitive with core offering
- [ ] Active development (commits in last 30 days or regular product updates)
- [ ] Genuine community (engagement rate > 1%, not purely inflated)

### Strong Signal (+score)
- [ ] Has publicly mentioned [YOUR_PROJECT] or adjacent problems
- [ ] Shared investors or advisors
- [ ] Explicitly building in same vertical ([e.g. DePIN, AI infrastructure, RWA])
- [ ] Community overlap (their users would benefit from our product)

### Disqualifiers
- [ ] Direct competitor to core product
- [ ] Project with known regulatory issues
- [ ] Token primarily positioned as speculative / no utility
- [ ] Team with history of rug or abandoned projects
