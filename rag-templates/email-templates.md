## Template: EXCHANGE

**Subject line options:**
- `[PROJECT NAME] × [EXCHANGE NAME] — Listing Discussion`
- `[TOKEN SYMBOL] listing — [EXCHANGE NAME]`

**Body:**
```
Hi [FIRST NAME],

[PERSONALIZATION: Reference one specific thing about the exchange — recent listing, product launch, or market position]

[PROJECT NAME] is [one-line description]. We've [specific traction metric — e.g. "processed X in compute volume" or "grown to X node operators"].

We'd love to explore a listing on [EXCHANGE NAME]. Here's what we bring:

• [Value point 1 — e.g. "Strong retail community: Xk holders, active on Twitter/TG"]
• [Value point 2 — e.g. "Institutional backing: [investors]"]
• [Value point 3 — e.g. "Real utility: token is required for network access, not speculative"]

Happy to share our listing deck and tokenomics. Would 20 minutes this week work?

[SIGNATURE]
```

## Template: PROTOCOL_PARTNER

**Subject line options:**
- `[PROJECT NAME] × [PARTNER] — Integration opportunity`
- `Building on [CHAIN/ECOSYSTEM]? Let's talk`

**Body:**
```
Hi [FIRST NAME],

[PERSONALIZATION: Reference their recent product update, blog post, or ecosystem announcement]

I'm [NAME] from [PROJECT NAME]. We're building [one-line description] on [chain/ecosystem].

I noticed [PARTNER] is [specific observation about their product]. We think there's a natural integration here:

[PROJECT NAME] could [specific integration value — e.g. "provide your dApp with decentralized compute at X% lower cost than centralized alternatives"].

A few projects already doing this: [1–2 real examples if available].

Worth a 20-minute call to explore? I can share our integration docs in advance.

[SIGNATURE]
```

**Body:**
```

[PERSONALIZATION: Reference a portfolio company or recent investment thesis post from their firm]

[PROJECT NAME] is [one-line description]. We're currently raising our [ROUND] round.

Key metrics:
• [Metric 1 — e.g. "ARR / revenue / TVL / nodes"]
• [Metric 2 — e.g. "Growth rate MoM"]
• [Metric 3 — e.g. "Key partnerships or backers"]

Given [FIRM]'s focus on [their stated thesis], we thought this might be relevant.

I've attached a brief deck. Happy to schedule a call if it's a fit.

[SIGNATURE]

**Body:**
```

[PERSONALIZATION: Reference their recent content — specific tweet, thread, or video title]

I'm [NAME] from [PROJECT NAME] — [one-line description].

Your audience cares about [relevant topic they cover]. We're building something directly in that space and think it would resonate:

[1–2 sentences on what makes your project interesting content — focus on story and novelty, not token price]

Would you be open to a quick intro call?

[SIGNATURE]
