## Banner Formats

### Twitter/X Header
- Size: 1500 × 500px
- Safe zone: Center 900px (edges may be cropped on mobile)
- Content: Logo + one-liner tagline + visual
- Tone: [e.g. "Minimal, technical, confident"]

### Twitter/X Post (with image)
- Size: 1200 × 675px (16:9)
- Content: Quote card or announcement
- Text on image: [e.g. "Headline only — no body text on image"]

### LinkedIn Banner
- Size: 1584 × 396px
- Content: Same as Twitter header style

### Discord Banner
- Size: 960 × 540px
- Content: Server branding + invite context
