## Significance Scoring Rules

When Data Agent evaluates a competitor activity, it uses these rules:

**Score 1 — FYI:**
- Routine tweets, minor product updates
- No strategic signal
- Action: log and include in weekly digest

**Score 2 — Notable:**
- New product feature launch
- Partnership announcement with a significant player
- Hiring surge (signal of growth)
- Action: include in weekly report with PM Agent flag

**Score 3 — Requires Response:**
- Direct attack on your project
- Major partnership that affects your market position
- Launch of a feature that directly overlaps your core offering
- Significant price movement that might be compared to yours publicly
- Action: immediate PM Agent alert + same-day strategy discussion
